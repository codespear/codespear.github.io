Detailed Description

A collision managers that resolves collisions using boxes.

Essentially the ground is divided into equally sized boxes. When checking for a collision related boxes are checked - not all objects in the manager. Keep in mind that an object may be in many boxes – because it is larger than a box, or on a box boundary.
